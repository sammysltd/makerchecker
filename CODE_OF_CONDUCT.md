# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct.

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. Be respectful and assume good faith.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
and discussions — and when an individual is representing the project in public
spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to **hello@makerchecker.ai**. All reports are
reviewed and investigated promptly, and the reporter's privacy is respected.
Maintainers who do not enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face
temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the
project's leadership.

The full text — including the enforcement guidelines and the ladder of
consequences — is in the linked Contributor Covenant v2.1.
